EVERY LIVING THING IS MADE OF WATER

In the field of Biology, the Holy Qur'an says in Surah Al Anbiya, Chapter No. 21, Verse No. 30:

“We have created every living thing from water - Will you not then believe?“

{{REF_AR=21:30}}

Imagine in the deserts of Arabia, where there was scarcity of water, the Qur'an says… Everything was created from water... who would have believed in it? Anything else the Qur'an would have said people could have believed. ‘Water’ - where there was scarcity - the Qur'an says 1400 years ago… ‘Every living thing is created from water’. Today we have come to know that Cytoplasm which is the basic substance of the living cell contains 80% water. Every living creature contains 50 to 70% water, and without water, the living creature cannot survive - It is a must.

Qur'an asks you… “We have created from water every living thing - Will you not then believe?”

Allah is saying… ‘that everything is created from water’ - which you came to know today. Qur'an mentions that 1400 years ago… “Will you not then believe?” It’s asking you a question, It wants a reply.

The Qur'an says in Surah Nur, Chapter No. 24, Verse No. 45, that:

“We have created every living animal from water.”

{{REF_AR=24:45}}

The Qur'an says in Surah Furqan, Chapter No. 25, Verse No. 54:
